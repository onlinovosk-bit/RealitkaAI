# FIRST CLIENT LAUNCH CHECKLIST (14 DNÍ) — REVOLIS.AI

**Cieľ:** byť pripravený na prvý platený enterprise klientsky onboarding bez právneho, prevádzkového a reputačného rizika.

---

## Deň 1–3 (Critical)

1. **Trust Center live + DPA request flow**
   - Owner: Legal Lead
   - Approver: CEO
   - Output: public legal pages + request formulár + response SLA.

2. **Billing E2E smoke**
   - Owner: CTO
   - Approver: CEO
   - Output: trial/pro/cancel/refund test report.

3. **Incident communication SOP**
   - Owner: COO
   - Approver: CTO
   - Output: P1 template, update cadence, postmortem template.

---

## Deň 4–7 (High)

4. **Support operating model**
   - Owner: CS Lead
   - Approver: COO
   - Output: support inbox, priority matrix, 1st response SLA.

5. **Data onboarding pack**
   - Owner: Product Ops
   - Approver: Sales Lead
   - Output: import templates, validation rules, 24h onboarding runbook.

6. **Monitoring baseline**
   - Owner: SRE/CTO
   - Approver: CTO
   - Output: uptime checks + 5xx alerts + auth/billing alerts.

---

## Deň 8–11 (Medium)

7. **Deliverability hardening (email/SMS)**
   - Owner: Growth/CRM Ops
   - Approver: CTO
   - Output: SPF/DKIM/DMARC + bounce handling + domain warmup.

8. **Security questionnaire pack**
   - Owner: Security Lead
   - Approver: Legal Lead
   - Output: predvyplnené odpovede pre procurement.

9. **Sales enablement legal kit**
   - Owner: Sales Lead
   - Approver: Legal Lead
   - Output: objection matrix, one-pager, approved answers.

---

## Deň 12–14 (Nice-to-have but strong)

10. **Versioned legal release rhythm**
    - Owner: Legal Ops
    - Approver: General Counsel
    - Output: legal changelog governance (monthly cadence).

11. **QBR + customer success framework**
    - Owner: CS Lead
    - Approver: COO
    - Output: 30/60/90 template a success metrics per tenant.

12. **Launch readiness sign-off**
    - Owner: CEO
    - Approver: Board/Founders
    - Output: final go/no-go table (Legal, Security, Product, Sales).
