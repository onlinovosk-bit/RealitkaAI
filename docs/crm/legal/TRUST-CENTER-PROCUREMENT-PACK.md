## 3. Security evidence checklist (to attach per deal)

- architektúrny diagram (high-level),
- data flow diagram (PII a subprocessori),
- posledný pen-test executive summary,
- incident response policy summary,
- backup/DR test summary (posledný test),
- access control policy summary (RBAC/MFA),
- vulnerability management summary.

---

## 4. Privacy/GDPR evidence checklist

- register subprocessors + SCC/TIA status,
- retention/deletion policy summary,
- DSAR workflow popis (roles + SLA),
- DPIA trigger matrix + posledné použitie,
- transfer impact controls (USA transfers),
- no-training-on-customer-data statement.

---

## 5. AI governance evidence checklist

- AI change log (posledných 6 mesiacov),
- model risk policy (black-box + explainability light),
- human oversight / kill switch SOP,
- AI incident taxonómia a incident templates,
- customer-facing AI disclaimer text.

---

## 6. Commercial readiness

- cenník s jasným splitom Base Fee / Performance Fee,
- service credit policy (SLA cap),
- redline fallback matrix (hard/soft positions),
- escalation matrix pre legal/security otázky.

---

## 7. Internal SLA for deal support

- prvý security/legal response: do 1 pracovného dňa,
- kompletný due diligence balík: do 3 pracovných dní,
- redline turnaround: do 2 pracovných dní (štandard),
- exec escalation: do 24 hodín.
